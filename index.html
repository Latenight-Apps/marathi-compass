Privacy Policy for Compass App

Effective Date: June 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Compass App, developed by Latenight Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Compass App ("the App"). The App provides a compass and level tool using device sensors, with direction labels in Hindi.

Information We Collect

The Compass App does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. The App operates entirely offline and uses only the following device sensors:

  • Accelerometer: To detect device orientation for the level feature.
  • Magnetometer: To determine direction for the compass feature.

No data from these sensors is stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties.

How We Use Information

The App uses sensor data solely to provide real-time compass and level functionality. This data is processed locally on your device and is not saved or sent to any external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, as no personal or sensor data is collected or stored by the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. It complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Security

As the App does not collect or store any data, there are no specific security measures required for data protection. The App operates securely within the Android system's permissions framework.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.
